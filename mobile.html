Hello World

My name is Michael Burnie, and this is my website. Welcome! I am a dedicated IT professional seeking additional knowledge and experience to achieve success in my field. The IT field is always expanding, and I plan to take advantage of any opportunity I can get. IT is also becoming a part of just about every field out there, and it's bringing everyone along by making technology accessible. I've always had a strong inclination towards technology and science, and IT is the culmination of those branches.

What am I doing?

See my ! Right now I am using my website to learn JavaScript and jQuery. I wish to experiment with several jQuery effects as well as plugins of which many generous developers have kindly provided online.

The following is a list of the plugins I am currently using or have used on this site previously:

  • Caleb Troughton's Waypoints
  • Jack Moore's jQuery Zoom
  • Trevor Davis' One Page Nav
  • Ariel Flesler's ScrollTo

Special thanks to Subtle Patterns, Google Fonts, and Responsive Slides.

I'm also looking into responsive web design. I find the simpler sites to be the most appealing, which is why I have everything on one page; however, as you can see from the state of this site, I don't really have an eye for design—I am a programmer after all. If you have ideas on how to improve my site, let me know!
